HealthVoice AI

Medical explanations in plain language, via phone or WhatsApp—no smartphone required.

🚨 The Problem

40% of elderly and low-income patients don't use smartphones regularly. They're left out of the healthcare AI revolution. Complex medical jargon creates confusion and poor health outcomes.

💡 Our Solution

HealthVoice AI bridges the digital divide by providing AI-powered medical explanations through the most accessible technologies: voice calls and WhatsApp/SMS.
